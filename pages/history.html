History

The History of My Writing

I had begun my writing in my freshmen year of high-school. At first, I was admittedly terrible but as I kept working on it throughout the years I will say that in my own opinion it got better. Some of the works I had written and looking at it with hindsight (and some forgetfulness) I like them.

Probably my best years thus far when it comes to my writing would be during my junior and senior year of high school, as I got some much writing it was invigorating! But as time went on and as I went on into college my rate of writing slowed down to an eventual crawl.

But just because I have been slowing down does not mean I am stopping. Just... sometimes a writer has to get into the mood or else they get writers block...

For a quick reference, here is a table of my "years" of learning.

Table of my intermediary years of writing.
Years Opinion of Writing
Freshmen / 2013 Beginning / Terrible
Freshmen - Junior / 2013-2016 Intermediate / Getting There.
Junior - Senior / 2016-2018 Some of my best years.
Senior - College / 2018-2021 Slow period but some quality work.
